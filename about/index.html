About Our Company

USE NATURALS SAVE NATURE NURTURE LIFE

In the business field for many years, Aseptica Natural Products Pvt Ltd has established its name among various industrial establishments in South India as suppliers of quality aromatics and natural oils for producing cosmetics, perfumes, soaps and personal care products. Formed as a company in 2015, the ASEPTICA is looking forward to expand its business horizons by entering into new fields and providing new services-both in terms of product diversity and increasing space coverage including export and import.The company has already

moved forward from the business of aromatics and is supplying quality products to various well established manufacturers of personal care and hygiene products like bath soaps, cleaning lotions hair and anti-bacterial oils. The company plans to expand its business into other areas like production, extraction, processing and marketing of spices and condiments, for which India is well known for centuries. The company also has plans to start agricultural farms for producing quality natural herbs, essential oils and spices.
