Cleaning Maid

Natural floor cleaner Aseptica cleaning maid is all purpose floor cleaning solution for house which kills germs and floors, walls and other part of your home. It serves also as a natural perfume or room spray.

How to Use

  1. For floor cleaning spray The Cleaning maid into a bucket of clean water. 10 sprays will be adequate. Wipe the floor Using a mop by dipping into the water. You floor will be crystal clean with added benefit of natural aroma.
  2. If you want to clean or disinfect, spray the cleaning maid onto the surface and wipe with a clean cloth.
